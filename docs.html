FishMasters Project

GitHub Repository: FishMasters on GitHub

Overview

FishMasters is a social and gamified mobile application tailored for fishing enthusiasts. The app offers a fun and interactive way to discover nearby fishing spots, log your catches, earn experience points, and compete with friends and other users.

Key Features

  • Nearby Water Bodies: Discover and explore fishing spots around your current location.
  • Catch Logging: Keep track of your catches with images, descriptions, and timestamps.
  • Experience System: Earn XP for each logged catch and climb the ranks.
  • Leaderboards: Compete globally or with friends to become the top angler.
  • Fishing Events: Create fishing events and keep in history your fishings.
  • Discoverable Locations: Be the first to find new spots and get rewarded as the pioneer angler.
